Concept:
--------
Ovelo Passport is a Flighty-style, highly visual, shareable "focus passport" summary.
It compresses a user's attention patterns into a futuristic passport/boarding-pass card.
It is designed to be exported as a 1080x1920 PNG for social media.

It should look premium, data-rich, and fun.

------------------------------------------------------------
1. OUTPUT FORMAT
------------------------------------------------------------
- React component inside app
- Export function: 1080x1920 PNG (vertical, social-media-friendly)

exportOveloPassportImage(data, options?) ‚Üí Blob
